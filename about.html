Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Naoman. I believe the web should be as carefully crafted as the products you sell.

My Philosophy

In a world of AI-generated code, drag-and-drop builders, and bloated frameworks, I've chosen a different path. I hand-code every website from scratch using the foundational languages of the web: HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript.

Why? Because your brand deserves more than a template. It deserves a digital storefront that loads in milliseconds, scores 100/100 on performance audits, is accessible to every visitor, and will never be compromised by a plugin vulnerability.

Just as you carefully source your ingredients, design your retreats, or craft your products — I carefully architect your digital presence. Every line of code is intentional. Every pixel is considered.

The Journey

I am currently on a rigorous 365-day roadmap to master full-stack web development and AI integration. I document every lesson, every bug, and every breakthrough publicly.

This isn't just about learning — it's about transparency. When you hire me, you're hiring someone who is relentlessly committed to their craft and openly shares their process.
